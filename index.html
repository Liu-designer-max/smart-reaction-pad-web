Clinical Interpretation Notes

  • RT artifacts: responses below 50 ms are treated as anticipations or sensor artifacts.
  • Delayed responses: responses above 2000 ms are excluded from mean RT and marked as missed/attention lapses.
  • LSI: 90% or above is commonly used as a functional symmetry target; below 85% is a deficit flag.
  • Dual-task cost: greater than 20% or more than 50 ms over baseline is flagged for caution.
  • Disclaimer: this dashboard supports education and prototype evaluation; it is not medical clearance.
